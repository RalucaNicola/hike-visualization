How difficult is it?

It is a medium difficulty hike which takes around 3.5 hours both ways. We did this hike in winter and we took our snowshoes, but the trail is well prepared and you can also do the hike without snowshoes. The day we went on the hike it was quite cold and windy closer to the lake, and that's why we turned around before actually getting to the lake.
